About Me

Welcome! My name is Hannah Boruchov.
I problem solve through design using my curiosity about how things work.

I'm a fourth-year student at Northwestern University majoring in Mechanical Engineering with a concentration in design. I'm also minoring in Art Theory and Practice, and completed the Segal Design Certificate. I seek to combine my love for design with my interests, which include product design, sustainability, automotive, aerospace, and biotechnology.
I utilize creativity to tackle challenging problems, employing both a big picture and detail oriented perspective. I value reliability, adaptability, and flexibility in the workplace and will bring my strong work ethic and organization to every situation.
In my spare time, I enjoy creating art, listening to music of all genres, exploring the outdoors during all seasons, and learning to cook unique vegetarian meals.
